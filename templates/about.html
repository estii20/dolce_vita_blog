About

This blog is created to share tips, suggestions and practical advice on all things food and drink related when visiting beautiful Venice.

Passionate about the topic and committed to providing accurate and reliable information.

What to Expect? In the cobblestone alleys and canal-side trattorias, this blog will be your guide to the finest cicchetti, sumptuous pasta dishes, and exquisite seafood that Venice has to offer. From the historic Rialto Market to the cozy bacari, we'll navigate the culinary landscape together, savoring each bite and sip along the way.

Why Venice? Venice isn't just a city; it's a gastronomic wonderland. The blog aims to celebrate the culinary heritageof this unique city, shedding light on the stories behind each dish and the passion that goes into crafting them. Expect not only reviews of the hottest eateries but also glimpses into the culture, history, and traditions that shape Venetian cuisine. So, whether you're planning a visit to this floating city or simply want to embark on a vicarious culinary adventure, join me on Venetian Bites & Sips for a taste of the extraordinary. Buon viaggio e buon appetito!

Any contribution is highly welcome. Feel free to get in touch for more information or to say hello.
